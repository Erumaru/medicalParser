Ankylosing spondylitis: diagnostic aspects and importance of NSAIDs in its treatment (tool to help general practitioners)
Bashkova I.B., Madyanov I.V

I.N. Ul'yanov Chuvash State University, Cheboksary, Russia

Summary
The causes of late diagnosis of ankylosing spondylitis (AS) are described. Diagnostic criteria for AS, specificity of inflammatory lower back pains, differential di-
agnostic signs of mechanical back pains, and indications for the referral of patients with chronic back pains to the rheumatologist are reviewed. When discussing
modern pharmacotherapy, special attention is given to non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs). The algorithm of NSAID choice which considers gas-
trointestinal and cardiovascular complications is described. Main aspects of the efficacy and safety of AS treatment with nimesulide are considered.
Keywords: ankylosing spondylitis, diagnostic criteria, non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, nimesulide.
